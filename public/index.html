What is HackTurabo?

HackPR Turabo's Student Chapter

HackTurabo is a student association at the Universidad del Turabo that seeks to promote the hacker and startup culture in students. We try to achieve our goal by giving workshops of topics that help the students to have skills that are not taught in the classroom. Also, we encourage our members to be part of a project of interest to help them expand their knowledge by working hands on. HackPR is providing resources and guidance to HackTurabo, so that the members can acquire experience and useful skills to be used in events. Our main goal is to have activities, such as: workshops, tech talks and our own small local hackathons.

HackPR: Organization

HackPR is a non-profit organization which is run by students. It originated in December of 2012 and it began with isolated hackathons that have now evolved into a more structured organization, with the purpose of expanding the hacker community in Puerto Rico.

Mision

Aspire to raise the standards and inspire the hacker culture in Puerto Rico, through the production of hackathons and other related activities, where students professionals and the general startup and tech communities can come together.
