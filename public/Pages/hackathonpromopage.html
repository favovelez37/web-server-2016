What is a Hackathon?

  • A hackathon (also known as a hack day, hackfest or codefest) is an event in which people with great ideas collaborate intensively to develop projects in a short amount of time. Normally, the participants are computer programmers and others involved in software development and hardware development, including graphic designers, interface designers and project managers.
  • Hackathons typically last between 12 hours to even three days! Some hackathons are intended simply for educational or social purposes, although in many cases, the goal is to create a product out of a simple idea.
  • Hackathons sometimes can have specific objectives and topicse like healthcare or finances or it could be to tinker with a specific platform like Arduino. There are also Hackathons that do not limit their hacks and accept any and all projects from its attendees.
